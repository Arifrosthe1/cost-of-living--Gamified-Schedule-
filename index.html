Brutal by design

The rules are simple. The consequences are not.

1
The daily tax never stops
RM5 deducted every midnight, regardless of what you did. Even on weekends. Even if you're sick. The cost of existing is non-negotiable.
2
Missed contracts compound your stress
A contract you made for today? If midnight passes, it's automatically failed and −RM15 is deducted. No extensions, no mercy.
3
Debt is a trap, not a floor
Once you go negative, 25% compounding interest fires each day before tax. At −RM100 on day 1, you're at −RM156 by day 3. The hole gets deeper, faster.
4
Bankruptcy is the nuclear option
If debt reaches −RM299, you can declare bankruptcy. All data wipes. All habits, rewards, and history gone. You start at −RM100 and type "I GIVE UP" to confirm.
