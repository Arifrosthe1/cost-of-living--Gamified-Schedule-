# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Productivity-minded individuals who seek high-stakes motivation to sustain discipline. They want to gamify their daily routine, using consequences (like debt compounding and tax deductions) as a forcing function for daily execution.

## Product Purpose
To introduce the brutal economic loop of "Cost of Living" and pull users directly into launching the live web application. The landing page must feel premium and high-stakes enough that users feel immediate urgency to try it.

## Brand Personality
Minimalist, premium, high-stakes. Serious, authoritative, and clean, rather than playful or cartoonish.

## Anti-references
- **AI Slop / SaaS Clichés**: No cards nested inside cards, no generic violet-to-blue gradient cards, no cute geometric illustrations or icons-in-boxes above headings.
- **Overused Aesthetics**: No saturated cream/beige default landing page backdrops.
- **Uncontrolled Motion**: No bouncy, elastic, or fast-jittering keyframe animations.

## Design Principles
1. **Consequence-Led Hierarchy**: Let the brutal mechanics (tax, compounding interest, bankruptcy) stand at the forefront of the copy and visuals.
2. **Atmospheric Minimalism**: A stark dark aesthetic with high typographic contrast, deep rich greens/reds, and generous negative space to feel premium.
3. **Cinematic Motion**: Every transition must feel heavy, deliberate, and smooth, using custom easing curves that mimic real physical momentum rather than instant snappiness.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Contrast ratio ≥ 4.5:1 for body and placeholder copy.
- Full respect for `prefers-reduced-motion` with clean crossfade fallbacks.
